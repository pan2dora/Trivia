How many did you get right?

  1. What color is a newborn panda?

    2. D. Pink

    Baby pandas are born pink, hairless, and only about the size of a stick of butter.

  2. How many species of Pandas are there?

    3. A. 2

    There are two species of pandas: the giant panda and the red panda, though they are not closely related.

  3. Do Pandas hibernate for the winter?

    4. B. No

    Unlike other bears, giant pandas do not hibernate. Instead, they move to lower elevations during winter to stay warm.

  4. What is a Panda's diet primarily made up of?

    5. B. Bamboo

    Pandas eat up to 99% bamboo, but their digestive system is still that of a carnivore!
